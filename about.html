About

How We Can Help

The expert immigration consultant team at Global Era Immigration provides full immigration services including, but not limited, to permanent residency, work permits, family sponsorship, student visas, visit visas and super visas. Global Era Immigration is known for providing personalized service and a unique understanding of immigration issues and how they affect people's lives. We bring skilled professionals and family members to Canada and help people just like you realize your dream of a better life here. We assist applicants to convert their immigration applications into Canadian permanent resident visas.We ensure our high level of professionalism and attention to detail is met with regard to each and every person applying for Canadian immigration.

Many of our clients receive Canadian permanent residence without even having to attend an immigration interview because their filed paperwork is already prepared by us in accordance with Citizenship and Immigration Canada's exact standards. We encourage you to fill out our free assessment form so we can assist in you in determining whether you areeligible for immigration to Canada and how Global Era Immigration might be able to offer assistance. We carefully review and consider all responses given by people who complete our convenient online Canada immigration eligibility assessment questionnaire.
