Express Entry
Express Entry is completely electronic process where applicant is assessed based on their skills, age, education, language ability, work experience, and many other factors will considered and score will be awarded using Comprehensive Ranking System ( CRS) which is a points based system. Application for this stream must be made through the Express Entry profile system on the CIC online portal. Having a Canadian job offer will give you more points thereby increasing your chance of receiving an invitation to apply.

PNP
The Provincial Nominee Programs (PNPs) allow Canadian provinces and territories to nominate individuals who wish to immigrate to Canada and who are interested in settling in a particular province. Each Canadian province (except Quebec, which has a different selection system) and two territories have their own unique Provincial Nominee Programs. Participating provinces and territories sign agreements with Immigration, Refugees and Citizenship Canada (IRCC) that allow them to select immigrants who meet the requirements that they have set forth.
